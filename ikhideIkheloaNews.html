Ikhide R. Ikheloa to receive James Currey Lifetime Achievement Award in Literary Criticism

25 August 2021


The Board of Advisors of the James Currey Society in cooperation with African Studies Centre, University of Oxford, has announced Mr. Ikhide Ikheloa will be honoured with the James Currey Lifetime Achievement Award for Literary Criticism. Mr. Ikheloa has been a consistent voice in appreciation of literary and artistic work.


This Award is to appreciate and recognize the important work he has done in Literary Criticism.The James Currey Society is focused on researching the works of eminent British publisher, James Currey.He will be honoured at the James Currey Prize Ceremony on September 3, 2022 at St. Antony’s College, University of Oxford. For more details, contact: info@jamescurreysociety.co.uk
