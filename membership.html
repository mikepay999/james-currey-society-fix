Membership Advantages

  • All members enjoy complimentary general admission for a full year, discounted general admission tickets for up to two guests per visit (NEW!), FREE admission to select screenings (NEW!), invitations to members-only programmes and special screenings, discounts at the James Currey Literary Festival, members-only viewing hours at the James Currey Exhibitions each month, expedited check-in, and other fantastic benefits like free admission to select screenings at the Oxford Afro-Caribbean film festival.

  • Each membership level offers expanded perks, and we are pleased to offer exciting new benefits for Contributor-level Members and up—complimentary general admission for two guests during each visit, two complimentary tickets to select events, and more!

  • Your membership allows you to engage with the Society in a truly special way, while also providing valuable support for the creative arts and helping us to create engaging, accessible programming and exhibitions.
