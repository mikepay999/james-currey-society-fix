About us

The James Currey Society is a nonprofit organization in coorperation with the African Studies Centre, University of Oxford. The Society is focused on studying the works done by British publisher, James Currey, making his contributions to African and Caribbean literature and publishing known globally. The Society achieves this through workshops, conferences, institution of prizes and residencies.
